Rijam's Mod is a bunch of random additions created by Rijam. Recipe Browser is highly recommended.

"Extemporaneous"

Includes:

[b] 3 Town NPCs & 1 Town Pet [/b]
[list]
[*] Harpy: sells sky related items and a few extra things. Find her in the sky.
[*] Interstellar Traveler: sells information items and weapons. Has a quest system. Arrives if the player has an Odd Device in their inventory (Post-Evil Boss).
[*] Hell Trader: sells Underworld related items. Spawns randomly in the Underworld, can move in in Hardmode.
[*] Snugget Town Pet: License sold after 60% Bestiary completion and Interstellar Traveler is present.
[/list]

[b] 241 new items and weapons throughout the game's progression [/b]
[list]
[*] 67 weapons: 12 Melee, 10 Ranged, 24 Magic, 22 Summoner.
[*] 13 ammo types.
[*] 44 accessories.
[*] 26 armor pieces.
[*] 13 vanity items + 3 dyes.
[*] 13 potions/food.
[/list]

[b] Some QoL changes [/b]
[list]
[*] Town NPCs sell more things.
[*] Some vanilla enemies drop their rare items more often.
[*] Interstellar Traveler sells information items.
[/list]

[b] Miscellaneous additions [/b]
[list]
[*] 8 new enemies.
[*] 11 tiles.
[*] Play pianos with the Pianist's Glove.
[*] Config to customize the mod.
[/list]

[b] Some cross mod support [/b]
[list]
[*] Support for Census - Town NPC Checklist.
[*] Harpy sells some items from other mods.
[*] Town NPC cross mod dialog and happiness.
[*] Bosses As NPCs support.
[/list]

The code is open source if you extract the mod!
